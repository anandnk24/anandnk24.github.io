About Me

I am an Economics and Computer Science student at the University of Chicago. I'm originally from the Bay Area.

I live at the intersection of technology, business, and innovation. I am eclectically curious, broad-minded and entrepreneurial - I'm interested in building ventures that change the world.

I'm a voracious reader and I love learning. I religiously follow basketball (go Warriors!). I occasionally jam on the piano and fanboy over Elon Musk (sometimes simultaneously).

When I'm not following tech blogs or learning, I am developing and hacking things and meeting exciting people in the process. I am incredibly passionate about technology and its ability to change the world - I aspire to be a part of that change.
